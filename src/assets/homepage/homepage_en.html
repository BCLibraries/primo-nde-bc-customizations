What am I searching?

Everything: includes many of the resources available from the BC Libraries including books, journals, newspapers, music, films, articles, dissertations, and more. This default search combines the Books and the Articles options.

Books: searches for books, database titles, journal/newspaper titles, video and audio content, music, data, etc. This finds both print and online items.

Articles: searches a large collection of online database content including articles, book chapters, dissertations, government documents, media, and open access content. This does not include all article content. For a more comprehensive search, use our Databases.
